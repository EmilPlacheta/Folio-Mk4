Mixer

Mixer is an JavaScript app that will suggest cocktails based on a type of spirit you have handy. After entering all spirits, app will 'mix it' and display all possible combinations for you!
This app, althogh simple and not fully finished (the 'database' needs expanding and result ideally would be links to a cocktail recipe) was a really fun project, that tought me a great deal about DOM manipulation and higher order functions.
During this project I've also used Sass and seen its benefits over regular CSS, and used it in all other projects since.
